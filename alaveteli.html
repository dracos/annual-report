Our Freedom of Information software can be used anywhere in the world

Right now Alaveteli is being used in 18 countries.

3 of those started up in 2014.

All of them make a difference

But some of them are in countries where
the Right To Know has become crucial.

Together, Alaveteli sites have enabled people to send more than 269,954 requests for information

They publish the requests and answers online for everyone to see

And even when there’s no reply…
…that is still a telling response
