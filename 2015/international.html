Poplus

500 people around the world are members of the Poplus community.

Its purpose?

To share civic technologies, so nobody has to keep on reinventing the wheel.

Some great things came out of Poplus this year…

YourNextRepresentative

After helping to build the crowdsourcing platform YourNextMP for the UK General Election, we remodelled it for international use.

It’s been busy collecting candidate data for Argentina ahead of their own election.

La Constitución De Todos

When Moroccans met Chileans at the first PoplusCon, they exemplified Poplus’ founding principles, reusing Legislation Lab’s software and enabling a countrywide discussion on the constitution for Chilean citizens.

EveryPolitician

What do you need before you can build a site like TheyWorkForYou or Mzalendo? Data. Lots of data.

To be specific, politician data. In a nice, clean, usable format.

That’s why we launched EveryPolitician, to store and share open, structured data on every politician in the world.

Yes, that’s right, we said every politician in the world.

Because what is life without a challenge?
