WhatDoTheyKnow is overseen and supported by our tireless group of volunteers: massive thanks to them.

You might have noticed that there was a General Election…

That kept us busy!

With other friendly organisations [LINK TO THEM] we set up YourNextMP, crowdsourcing information on every candidate for the General Election.

We believe that information was the earliest, most complete dataset to be released.

It was viewed by over a million people, looking for information about their voting choices.

It included contact details and links to social media profiles and was even used by Google in the UK to power a tailored election search [LINK HERE].

We changed all the MPs on TheyWorkForYou, in real time.
