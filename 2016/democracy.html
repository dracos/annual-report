Democracy

We help people in 12 countries run democracy sites: sites that keep an eye on their parliaments, publish out debates and votes, match constituents with their representatives, and show information about candidates where there are elections.

Meanwhile, here in the UK, we run two useful democracy sites: TheyWorkForYou, for keeping an eye on your elected representatives, and WriteToThem for letting them know how you feel about their activity.

But this year we’ve also been working on a massive initiative: the EveryPolitician project collects and shares data on politicians around the world so that anyone, anywhere, can use it for their own parliamentary monitoring site… or any other project.
